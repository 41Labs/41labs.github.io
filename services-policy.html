Services Policy

  1. Home
  2. Services Policy

1. Service Delivery

41Labs is committed to delivering high-quality AI and technology solutions. Our services are provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis. We strive to maintain the highest standards of service delivery and professional conduct throughout our engagement with clients.

2. Project Scope and Timeline

All projects begin with a clearly defined scope of work and timeline. Any modifications to the project scope must be agreed upon in writing. We work closely with our clients to establish realistic timelines and milestones while maintaining flexibility to accommodate changing requirements.

3. Payment Terms

Our payment terms are project-specific and will be outlined in the service agreement. Typically, we require:

  • An initial deposit before work begins
  • Milestone payments based on project progress
  • Final payment upon project completion
All invoices are payable within the terms specified in the service agreement.

4. Intellectual Property Rights

Unless otherwise specified in the service agreement:

  • Clients retain ownership of their pre-existing intellectual property
  • 41Labs maintains ownership of pre-existing and independently developed intellectual property
  • Custom solutions developed specifically for clients are transferred upon full payment

5. Service Level Agreements

Our service level agreements (SLAs) specify:

  • Response times for support requests
  • System availability and performance metrics
  • Maintenance windows and update schedules
  • Resolution times for different issue priorities

6. Data Security and Privacy

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect client data. This includes:

  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with relevant data protection regulations
  • Strict access controls and authentication measures

7. Quality Assurance

Our quality assurance process includes:

  • Rigorous testing throughout development
  • Regular code reviews and quality checks
  • Performance optimization
  • Documentation and knowledge transfer

8. Support and Maintenance

Post-deployment support and maintenance services include:

  • Bug fixes and issue resolution
  • Regular updates and improvements
  • Technical support during business hours
  • Optional 24/7 support packages

9. Service Termination

Either party may terminate services with written notice as specified in the service agreement. Upon termination:

  • All outstanding payments become due immediately
  • Client data will be returned or destroyed as requested
  • Transition assistance may be provided if specified

10. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes will be resolved through:

  • Initial good-faith negotiations
  • Mediation if necessary
  • Arbitration as a last resort

For specific inquiries about our services policy, please contact our support team.
